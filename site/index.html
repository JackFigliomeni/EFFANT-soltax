Anatomy of a wallet

What a real Solana wallet is made of.

Eighteen months of one real wallet, classified by SOLTAX. Hover the chart — more than half of it never belonged in a tax report.

    Method

    Deterministic, resumable, honest.

    01

    Fetch once, keep forever

    Full parsed history streams to a local, signature-keyed cache. Interrupted runs resume from a cursor; a transaction is never fetched twice.

    02

    Net the flows

    Every event is derived from what actually moved in and out of your accounts — so multi-hop routes collapse and wash legs cancel to zero.

    03

    Refuse to guess

    Anything unrecognized is marked UNKNOWN and lands on a worklist with Solscan links — never silently mislabeled. This wallet’s worklist: empty.
